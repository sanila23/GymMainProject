Body buiding

Bodybuilding is a form of physical fitness training focused on developing and sculpting muscle mass through resistance exercises, nutrition, and rest. It involves targeted workouts aimed at increasing muscle size, strength, and definition. Bodybuilders often follow strict dietary regimens and training schedules to achieve their desired physique, which may include competitions showcasing muscular aesthetics and symmetry.

Trainer

A gym trainer is a fitness professional responsible for guiding individuals in their exercise routines and overall fitness goals. They assess clients' fitness levels, develop personalized workout plans, demonstrate proper exercise techniques, and provide motivation and support to help clients achieve desired results. Trainers also educate clients on nutrition, injury prevention, and lifestyle changes to enhance overall health and well-being.
